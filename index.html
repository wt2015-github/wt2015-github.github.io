Research Interests  Top

Interested in many topics on Bioinformatics, Biostatistics, Genetics, Network and Systems Biology, especially:
  • Integrative analysis of high-throughput and multi-omics biological data to interpret the development and progression of complex diseases, and to identify molecular biomarkers or therapeutic targets for disease diagnosis or treatment.
  • Next generation sequencing (RNA-seq, Chip-seq, ATAC-seq, bisulfite sequencing, small-RNA sequencing, single cell sequencing) and microarray (gene expression, DNA methylation) data analysis.
  • Systems and network analysis, pathway analysis.
  • Design and application of algorithms in Bioinformatics.
  • Machine learning, deep learning and application.
